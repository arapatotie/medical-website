Our Mission

MIMS ph provides accurate, up-to-date, and comprehensive drug information and clinical resources to support healthcare professionals in making informed decisions for their patients. We are committed to advancing healthcare through reliable medical information.

Our platform serves as a bridge between medical research and clinical practice, ensuring that healthcare providers have access to the latest evidence-based information when they need it most.

Our History

2024 — This MIMS PH edition is focused exclusively on the Philippines. In 2024 we updated the local platform to align content with Philippine clinical practice, formularies, and regulatory guidance. The Philippine edition consolidates global MIMS expertise with locally relevant information to better support Filipino healthcare professionals.

Editorial Policy

All content is developed and reviewed by our team of medical editors, pharmacists, and physicians. We adhere to strict editorial standards to ensure the accuracy and relevance of our information. Our content development process includes:

  • Regular updates based on latest clinical evidence
  • Peer review by subject matter experts
  • Compliance with FDA and international guidelines
  • Transparency in source attribution
  • Regular audit of content accuracy

Partnerships

MIMS ph collaborates with leading medical societies, academic institutions, and healthcare organizations to ensure our content meets the highest standards. Our partners include the American Medical Association, American Pharmacists Association, and various specialty societies across different medical disciplines.

We also work closely with pharmaceutical manufacturers to provide accurate and up-to-date drug information, while maintaining strict editorial independence.
