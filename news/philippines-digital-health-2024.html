Philippines advances digital health interoperability pilot to link hospital EMRs

Overview

A coalition of public and private hospitals, together with health IT vendors, has launched a pilot program in 2024 to test electronic medical record (EMR) interoperability based on HL7/FHIR profiles.

Key Components

The pilot focuses on exchanging:

  • Discharge summaries
  • Laboratory results
  • Medication lists

Project Goals

The primary objectives include:

  • Reducing duplication of medical tests
  • Improving continuity of care for patients moving between facilities
  • Streamlining referral processes

Early Results

Local clinical informatics teams report early gains in referral coordination. The success has prompted plans for expansion to additional regions, pending evaluation of:

  • Privacy frameworks
  • Patient consent workflows
  • Network governance structures

Future Plans

The initiative plans to expand its scope in phases, with the next phase including:

  • Integration with more healthcare facilities
  • Additional data types for exchange
  • Enhanced security protocols
  • Improved patient access mechanisms
